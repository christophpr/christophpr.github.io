Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Sarah Johnson and Michael Chen, Social Marketing Theresia has grown from a small two-person operation into a leading digital marketing agency serving clients across multiple industries.

With their combined expertise in creative direction and data-driven strategy, our founders have established a company culture that balances innovative design with measurable results. Their vision continues to guide our approach as we help businesses transform their digital presence and connect with their audience in meaningful ways.

Today, our diverse team of specialists works collaboratively to deliver comprehensive marketing solutions that drive growth and create lasting impact for our clients.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with businesses of all sizes across various industries, from startups to established enterprises. Our approach is tailored to each client's unique needs, whether you're looking to launch a new brand or revitalize an existing one. We have particular expertise in technology, sustainable products, lifestyle brands, and professional services.

How do you measure the success of marketing campaigns?

We believe in data-driven marketing and set clear KPIs at the start of every project. Depending on your goals, we might track website traffic, conversion rates, lead generation, social engagement, or ROI. Our team provides regular reports with actionable insights and continuously optimizes campaigns for maximum performance.

What is your typical process for new clients?

Our process typically begins with a discovery session to understand your business goals, target audience, and current marketing efforts. We then develop a customized strategy, present it for your feedback, and refine before implementation. Throughout the project, we maintain open communication and provide regular updates on progress and results.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

Absolutely! We offer various retainer packages for ongoing marketing support. Many clients choose to work with us long-term to maintain consistency in their marketing efforts and adapt strategies as their business evolves. We can handle everything from content creation and social media management to analytics and campaign optimization.

How long does a typical project take to complete?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A website redesign might take 8-12 weeks, while a comprehensive branding project could take 3-4 months. Social media campaigns can often be launched within 2-3 weeks. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline tailored to your specific project needs and priorities.
